Paulig

Paulig is a Nordic coffee company founded in Finland in 1876 and known for premium roasted coffee and sustainable sourcing practices. Paulig coffee blends are designed to match Scandinavian coffee traditions with smooth and balanced flavor profiles. The brand focuses on responsible coffee production, quality roasting, and environmentally conscious business practices. Paulig remains one of the leading coffee brands in Northern Europe.
